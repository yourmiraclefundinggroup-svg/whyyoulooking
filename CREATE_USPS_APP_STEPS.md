# Create Your USPS App - Step by Step

## What You're Seeing
Perfect! You're in the USPS Developer Portal Apps section. It's empty because you haven't created an app yet.

## Next Steps:

### 1. Create New App
Look for one of these buttons on your screen:
- **"Create New App"**
- **"New Application"**  
- **"Add App"**
- **"+"** (plus sign)
- **"Create"**

**Tap that button** to start creating your app.

### 2. Fill Out the App Form
You'll see a form asking for:

**Required Fields:**
- **App Name**: `ScoreShift Credit Repair`
- **Description**: `Credit repair platform for tracking certified dispute letters`
- **Website/URL**: `https://scoreshiftapp.com`
- **Contact Email**: [your business email]

**Optional Fields (if shown):**
- **Category**: Business/Finance
- **Environment**: Production
- **App Type**: Web Application

### 3. Submit the Form
- Click **"Submit"**, **"Create"**, or **"Save"**
- Wait for confirmation message

### 4. Get Your Credentials
After creating the app, you should see:
- **Consumer Key** (copy this - it's your USPS_CLIENT_ID)
- **Consumer Secret** (copy this - it's your USPS_CLIENT_SECRET)

## If You Don't See a "Create" Button

Try these:
1. **Scroll down** - the button might be below
2. **Check the top right** - look for "+ New" or similar
3. **Try the menu** - look for "Applications" or "My Apps"
4. **Refresh the page** - sometimes it needs a reload

## What to Send Me

Once you create the app and get the credentials, just paste them like this:
```
USPS_CLIENT_ID: [your consumer key here]
USPS_CLIENT_SECRET: [your consumer secret here]
```

That's it! I'll activate real USPS tracking immediately.
