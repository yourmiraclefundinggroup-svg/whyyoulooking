REPLIT PROMPT 2 — Build ScoreShift Concierge Smart Contract System

Build a Smart Contract system for ScoreShift Concierge clients.

Goal:
Every Concierge client receives a personalized contract inside the app based on their selected package, payment option, state, and client situation.

Concierge Packages:

1. Fast-Track — $800
   Flexible Pay:

   * $400 today
   * $200 in 2 weeks
   * $200 in 4 weeks
     Includes 3 months of Momentum after service completion.

2. Fast-Track Rush — $1,500
   Flexible Pay:

   * $750 today
   * $375 in 2 weeks
   * $375 in 4 weeks
     Includes 6 months of Momentum after service completion.

3. Fast-Track Elite — $2,500
   Flexible Pay:

   * $1,250 today
   * $625 in 2 weeks
   * $625 in 4 weeks
     Includes 12 months of Momentum after service completion.
     Includes Premium Tradeline Network Access and one Authorized User Tradeline.

Build flow:

Package selection
↓
Payment option selection
↓
Client intake details
↓
Contract generation
↓
Client review
↓
E-signature
↓
Payment
↓
Concierge dashboard unlocks

Contract should dynamically include:

* Client name
* Client email
* Client address
* State
* Selected package
* Total price
* Payment option
* Payment schedule
* Services included
* Rush status if applicable
* Tradeline language if Elite
* Momentum access after completion
* Concierge service terms
* Client responsibilities
* No guarantee disclaimer
* Authorization to review credit information
* Electronic signature consent

Important Concierge rules:

During active Concierge service, the client can view progress, milestones, status updates, monitoring data, and case activity.

Dispute IQ editing, letter creation, and dispute execution must remain locked while Concierge service is active.

After Concierge completion:

* Fast-Track unlocks 3 months of Momentum
* Rush unlocks 6 months of Momentum
* Elite unlocks 12 months of Momentum
* Dispute IQ unlocks
* Smart Letters unlock

Create admin controls:

* Generate contract
* View signed contract
* Mark contract signed
* Mark service active
* Mark service complete
* Unlock Momentum access
* Upload signed PDF copy if needed

Design should feel premium and trustworthy, like a fintech contract portal, not a generic form.
