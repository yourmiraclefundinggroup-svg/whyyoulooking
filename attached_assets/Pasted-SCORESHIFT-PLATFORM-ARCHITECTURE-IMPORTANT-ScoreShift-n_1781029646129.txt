ScoreShift now operates under two distinct customer experiences:

---

1. ScoreShift Self-Service

---

ScoreShift Self-Service is the software platform experience.

Customers use ScoreShift tools and guidance independently.

Self-Service users have access to platform tools based on their plan level.

* Financial Readiness Score

* Credit Monitoring
* Credit Reports
* Dispute IQ
* Smart Letters
* Debt Navigator
* Score Simulator
* Student Loan Aid
* Identity Monitoring
* Privacy Protection
* Subscription Manager
* Mortgage Readiness
* Funding Readiness

Dispute IQ is fully available according to plan permissions.

---

2. ScoreShift Concierge

---

ScoreShift Concierge is the premium managed-service experience.

The ScoreShift team performs the work and manages the strategy.

Concierge clients do NOT directly use Dispute IQ while service is active.

Instead they receive:

* Concierge Dashboard
* Progress Tracking
* Milestones
* Status Updates
* Monitoring Data
* Case Activity
* Team Updates
* Payment Tracking
* Contract Access

During active Concierge service:

Dispute IQ must remain locked.

Users can see what ScoreShift is doing but cannot:

* Create letters
* Edit letters
* Send disputes
* Execute dispute actions

Those actions remain managed by the Concierge team.

## Concierge Completion Logic

When Concierge service is completed:

Fast-Track:
Unlock 3 months Momentum

Rush:
Unlock 6 months Momentum

Elite:
Unlock 12 months Momentum

Upon completion:

* Dispute IQ unlocks
* Smart Letters unlock
* Momentum tools unlock
* Self-Service experience activates

This transition must happen automatically.

The customer moves from Concierge to Self-Service without creating a new account.
