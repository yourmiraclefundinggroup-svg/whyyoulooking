We are refining the ScoreShift design system and visual identity based on synthetic persona testing and brand psychology feedback.

The strongest validated foundation system is:

* Bone Ledger
* Shale Taupe
* Aura Indigo
* Muted Sage
* restrained Clay Apricot

The personas consistently responded positively to the emotional balance of:

* calmness
* authority
* operational structure
* warmth
* premium restraint
* progress
* emotional safety

IMPORTANT:
The emotional territory is:
“calm financial confidence.”

NOT:

* dark fintech
* crypto
* startup hype
* aggressive AI aesthetics
* loud SaaS visuals

---

## CRITICAL BRAND INSIGHT

The personas said:

“Bone Ledger + Shale Taupe + Aura Indigo is the correct foundation system.”

They specifically responded positively because the system felt:

* premium
* trustworthy
* calm
* intelligent
* differentiated

HOWEVER:

The personas also identified that Aura Indigo should:

* dominate interactions
* dominate progress moments
* dominate emotional movement

WITHOUT:

* becoming the full environment/background color.

This distinction is VERY important.

---

## UPDATED COLOR STRATEGY

FOUNDATION ENVIRONMENT:

1. Bone Ledger

Bone Ledger should remain the dominant environmental color.

Use for:

* backgrounds
* whitespace
* page atmosphere
* large surfaces
* editorial warmth

Purpose:

* calmness
* openness
* sophistication
* premium softness
* emotional relief

Suggested direction:
#F1E8DA
#F3EEE6
#EFE9DF

This should replace harsh white backgrounds.

The site should feel:

* tactile
* warm
* architectural
* calm
* human

NOT:

* sterile SaaS white
* cold fintech minimalism

---

STRUCTURAL DEPTH:
2. Shale Taupe

Use for:

* navigation
* typography support
* deep cards
* framing
* footers
* structure
* hierarchy

Purpose:

* authority
* seriousness
* operational trust
* financial credibility

Suggested direction:
#3A3734
#4A4541
#5B5652

IMPORTANT:
Shale Taupe should create selective moments of depth.
Do NOT make the entire interface dark.

This is the “trust structure” color.

---

PRIMARY BRAND COLOR:
3. Aura Indigo

IMPORTANT UPDATE:

We DO want stronger Aura Indigo ownership.

The personas validated the system but the brand still needs:

* recognizability
* memorability
* emotional signature
* stronger ScoreShift ownership

Therefore:
Aura Indigo should appear MORE frequently than before.

BUT:
Aura Indigo should NOT become the environmental foundation.

Instead:
Aura Indigo should become:

* the interaction color
* the movement color
* the progress color
* the intelligence color

Use Aura heavily in:

* CTA buttons
* progress indicators
* graphs
* score movement
* onboarding
* hover states
* active states
* charts
* onboarding transitions
* focus moments
* notifications
* key emotional highlights

Purpose:

* movement
* intelligence
* optimism
* financial progress
* clarity
* product memorability

Suggested direction:
#B0A6DF
#9F93D9
#8D80D3
#7C6BCB

The user should psychologically associate:
“progress”
with:
Aura Indigo.

That is the strategic goal.
