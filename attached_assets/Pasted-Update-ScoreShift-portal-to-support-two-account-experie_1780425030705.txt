SELF-SERVICE EXPERIENCE

Self-service users are driving their own process.

Primary question:
“What should I do next?”

Self-service homepage should focus on:

* Goal
* Readiness percentage
* Next Best Action
* Recommended ScoreShift tool
* Credit Snapshot
* Priority Actions
* Progress

Self-service users should be routed to:

* Dispute IQ for dispute-related issues
* Debt Navigator for utilization/debt issues
* Protection Center for identity/privacy issues
* Credit Report for scores/report/alerts/simulator
* Subscription Manager for recurring expense optimization
* Student Loan Aid for student loan-related opportunities
