SCORESHIFT PORTAL BACKGROUND IMPLEMENTATION

We have a premium cinematic background video that represents the ScoreShift brand world.

The video should function as an ambient environment behind the portal, not as a hero video or distracting animation.

Design Goals

Create a luxury fintech experience inspired by:

Apple
Linear
Notion
Arc Browser
Aesop

The video should feel like an environmental layer that adds atmosphere and emotional trust.

The portal content must always remain the primary focus.

Background Video Requirements

Use the supplied MP4/WebM video as a full-screen background.

Requirements:

autoplay
muted
loop
playsinline
no controls
fixed position
full viewport coverage
responsive on desktop and mobile

The video should remain behind all content.

Visual Treatment

Apply a soft overlay above the video:

Background overlay:

rgba(255,252,246,0.55)

This should slightly wash out the video and blend it into the ScoreShift design system.

The goal is:

calm
premium
readable
trustworthy

NOT:

cinematic trailer
distracting motion background
Motion Treatment

Video opacity:

Desktop:

opacity: 0.18;

Mobile:

opacity: 0.12;

Motion should feel almost subconscious.

Users should notice the environment after several seconds, not immediately.

Performance

Requirements:

lazy load video
use WebM when available
fall back to MP4
GPU acceleration enabled
no layout shifts
no performance degradation

Target:

Lighthouse 90+
Smooth scrolling
No dropped frames
Layer Structure

Layer 1:
Background video

Layer 2:
Cream atmospheric overlay

Layer 3:
Subtle glass blur layer

Layer 4:
Portal content

Glass Layer

Create a subtle glass layer above the video:

backdrop-filter: blur(8px);

Very light.

Do not create a frosted glass effect.

Only enough to soften the moving terrain behind content cards.

Dashboard Cards

Cards should feel elevated from the environment.

Card styling:

background: rgba(255,255,255,.82);
backdrop-filter: blur(12px);
border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,.45);
box-shadow: 0 12px 40px rgba(0,0,0,.08);
border-radius: 24px;
ScoreShift Brand Feel

The portal should feel like:

"A guided journey through financial clarity."

The moving landscape represents:

trust
intelligence
financial progress
guidance

Avoid:

crypto aesthetics
gaming aesthetics
sci-fi aesthetics
traditional fintech blue dashboards

Everything should feel warm, premium, human, and sophisticated.

Final Objective

Transform the portal from a standard SaaS dashboard into a premium immersive ScoreShift experience where the environment subtly communicates progress, trust, and financial transformation without distracting from the user's data.
