# REPLIT IMPLEMENTATION PROMPT

You are implementing the ScoreShift Pricing Experience.

The attached Figma file is the single source of truth.

Your job is NOT to redesign, reinterpret, simplify, modernize, or improve the experience.

Your job is to recreate the Figma as accurately as possible.

---

## Design Philosophy

ScoreShift is not a credit monitoring company.

ScoreShift is a financial guidance platform.

The pricing experience should feel like choosing a path forward, not buying software.

The emotional progression is:

```text
Clarity
↓
Choice
↓
Confidence
↓
Commitment
```

Not:

```text
Features
↓
Pricing
↓
Checkout
```

---

## Global Visual Direction

Use the existing ScoreShift design language already established throughout the landing page.

Maintain:

* Bone Ledger tones
* Ceramic White surfaces
* Warm cream environments
* Aura Indigo accents
* Soft shadows
* Premium spacing
* Museum-quality visual presentation

The experience should feel like:

```text
Apple
+
Linear
+
Luxury Hospitality
+
Private Banking
```

Never:

```text
Credit Karma
Credit Repair Website
Crypto Dashboard
Traditional SaaS Pricing Table
```

Do not replace imagery.
