--- DISPUTES PAGE (DIY only) ---
  Page hero: "Dispute IQ" — "Powered by ScoreShift"
  
  The Dispute IQ bot workflow inside this page:

  STEP 1 — ANALYZE
    Full credit report pulled via Array integration (no Array branding).
    Bot card: "Dispute IQ is analyzing your credit report..."
    Results card shows:
      - List of flagged negative items
      - For each item: violation type (Metro 2 | FCRA), severity, 
        recommended action
      - Bureau(s) affected shown as color pills

  STEP 2 — BUILD LETTERS
    For each flagged item, a dispute letter is auto-generated.
    Letter preview card: shows letter content, client name/address pre-filled.
    Up to 3 letters generated based on findings.
    Status tracker: Letter 1 ✓ | Letter 2 ✓ | Letter 3 ✓

  STEP 3 — SEND OR PRINT
    Two options per letter:
    
    Option A — "Send via Certified Mail" (LOB integration)
      Gold primary button: "Send via ScoreShift Mail — $X"
      Pricing based on plan:
        • $29.99 plan: pay per letter, shown price before confirming
        • $79.99 plan: first letter free (credit applied), 
          "1 credit used" shown, pay for additional
        • Elite plan: 2 free letter credits, pay for additional
      Confirmation modal: shows address, price, credit used — 
      "Confirm & Send" gold button.
      After send: tracking number from LOB displayed in --gold-light, copyable.
    
    Option B — "Print Letter"
      Ghost button: "Download & Print"
      Generates printable PDF version.
    
    Plan credit tracker at top of page:
      "Credits remaining: X / X" — gold badge
      "Upgrade for more credits" link if at 0.
