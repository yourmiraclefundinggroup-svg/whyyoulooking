PIXELATED PORTRAIT ANIMATIONS

The animation must be subtle and premium.

DO NOT use:

* spinning
* bouncing
* scaling
* floating avatars
* flashy effects
* fintech gimmicks

Instead:

1. Portrait Fade Reveal

As each portrait enters the viewport:

* opacity 0 → 100%
* slight upward movement (12px)
* duration 0.8s
* stagger 120ms

2. Dot Resolution Effect

When hovering:

The halftone pattern subtly sharpens and increases detail.

It should feel like the image is coming into focus.

Duration:
400ms ease-out.

3. Ambient Grain Motion

Apply an extremely subtle animated grain texture.

Barely noticeable.

Luxury editorial feel.

4. Slow Parallax

Portrait group moves at approximately 5% slower than scroll speed.

Very subtle.

Should feel cinematic.

5. Editorial Hover

On hover:

* increase contrast 5%
* increase sharpness slightly
* reduce paper texture slightly

The portrait should feel like it becomes more alive.

No zooming.

No dramatic movement.
